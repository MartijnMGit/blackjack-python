How Blackjack Works

• You and the computer each start with 2 cards.
• Click **Hit** to draw another card, or **Stand** to stop.
• Going over 21 = bust → you lose.
• Closest to 21 without busting wins.
• Aces count as 11 or 1 — whichever helps most.
